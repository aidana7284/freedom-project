What is a serial killer?

A serial killer is usually defined as a person who has killed 3 or more people with the murders taking place over the span of a month or more.

Why do serial killers kill?

Generally serial killers kill people for psychological gratification however according to the FBI the motives of serial killers can include anger, thrill-seeking, financial gain, and attention seeking.

How common are serial killers?

According to the FBI serial killers account for less than 1% of murder victims in the U.S.
